Our Story

Quality, Hygiene & Satisfaction

Located in the scenic Ganpatipule, Tarang Restaurant is dedicated to bringing you the finest flavors of Indian and Chinese cuisine. We believe that great food starts with quality ingredients and a passion for cooking.

Whether you are a local resident or a traveler visiting the holy temple, our mission is to provide a hygienic, welcoming environment where every meal is a celebration. From our signature Malwani specialties to classic Chinese favorites, we ensure every dish meets the highest standards of taste and freshness.
